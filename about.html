About Me

Philosophy, Technology, and Creative Expression

My Story

Hello, I'm Boran Birtanır. I'm a thinker, creator, and explorer at heart. My journey has been shaped by a deep fascination with the big questions of existence, consciousness, and human potential.

With a background spanning technology and philosophy, I've dedicated myself to exploring how human creativity and technological innovation intersect. I believe we can harness technology to enhance rather than diminish our humanity by understanding both technical and philosophical dimensions.

My interests include AI-generated art, philosophical explorations of consciousness, and finding meaningful balance in our increasingly digital lives. Through this website, I share my journey, insights, and creative explorations.

My Philosophy

Consciousness as Primary

I'm intrigued by idealist philosophy—the notion that consciousness might be more fundamental than matter. This perspective opens new ways of understanding our place in the universe.

Mindful Technology

Technology should serve human flourishing, not diminish it. I explore ways to engage with digital tools mindfully, using them as extensions of our creativity rather than replacements for it.

Embracing Uncertainty

I believe that embracing uncertainty is essential for growth and discovery. By remaining open to the unknown, we create space for wonder and new possibilities.
