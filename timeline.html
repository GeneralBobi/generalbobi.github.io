My Journey

Key moments and milestones that have shaped my path

Life is a continuous journey of exploration and growth. This timeline captures some of the pivotal moments in my personal and intellectual development—from early philosophical discoveries to creative experiments with emerging technologies. Each experience has contributed to my evolving perspective on consciousness, creativity, and human potential.

2012

First Encounter with Eastern Philosophy

Discovered the works of Alan Watts and began exploring Buddhist and Taoist concepts that fundamentally shifted my understanding of consciousness and reality.

Philosophy Consciousness
2015

Deep Dive into Technology

Began studying programming and digital design, fascinated by the potential of technology to expand human creativity and problem-solving capabilities.

Technology Creativity
2018

Beginning of Digital Minimalism

After noticing the impact of constant connectivity on my attention and wellbeing, I began experimenting with intentional digital habits and regular technology fasts.

Mindfulness Digital Balance
2020

AI Art Exploration

Started experimenting with AI art tools like Stable Diffusion, exploring the fascinating boundary between human creativity and machine intelligence.

AI Art
2021

Idealist Philosophy Deep Dive

Discovered Bernardo Kastrup's work on idealism, which resonated deeply with my intuitions about consciousness being fundamental rather than emergent from matter.

Philosophy Consciousness
2022

Music Composition with AI

Began experimenting with AI music generation tools like Suno, creating compositions that blend human emotional intent with algorithmic patterns.

Music AI
2023

Digital Detox Journey

Embarked on a practice of regular digital detox periods, seeking to cultivate a healthier relationship with technology while preserving its benefits.

Wellbeing Balance
2024

Personal Website Launch

Created this digital space to share my journey, creative explorations, and philosophical reflections with a wider community of like-minded explorers.

Web Creativity
